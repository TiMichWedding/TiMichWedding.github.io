The Beginning

It all started with a simple and polite email exchange after an introduction from family & friends. Michelle, as popular as she was, more or less ignored Tim :-P. Tim was Sr. Software Dev Manager in Seattle and Michelle was a lawyer living in Shanghai. Neither was looking for a long-distance relationship.

Sept 11th, 2019

First Meeting

Tim was visiting Taipei for his brother’s wedding. On a very rainy day stuck in the apartment, boredom drove Tim to randomly text Michelle who happened to be visiting her family in Tainan. After some convincing, Tim hopped on a 2-hour train ride to meet up and spend a day with Michelle.

Toward the end of the day, the two briefly stopped by a stand where the grandma “mistakenly” told them to hurry up and get married. Turns out grandma was actually a clairvoyant.

Travel Info

From Taoyuan airport (TPE): Taxi is the easiest and reasonably-priced way to get to Taipei. There is a taxi info station as you exit the baggage claim area. The cost should be around ~1400 NT Dollars. For a cheaper option, you can take the Taoyuan Airport MRT to Taiwan Main Station for ~150 NT Dollar. Try to take the purple Express train as it'll be much faster.

From Taipei Main Station: You should be able to get to where you need by taxi for a few hundred NT dollars. If you prefer to take MRT, you can get to the wedding by taking the Blue line toward Nangang, and get off at Zhongxiao Xinsheng station (2 stops). To get to Shangrai-La hotel, continue on the Blue line to Zhongxiao Fuxing station. Transfer to the Brown line toward Taipei Zoo Station, and get off at either Technology Building or Liuzhangli station. (Metro Map)

Hotel options:

  • Shangri-La Far Eastern (where the reception will be held)
  • Howard Plaza Hotel (old, but conveniently located)
